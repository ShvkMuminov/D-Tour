About D-Tour

D-tour was established in 2014 and has more than 10 years experience in inbound and outbound tourism. During this 10 years D-tour company is succeeded to offer it's services in main 3 cities of Uzbekistan. Tashkent, Andijan, and Namangan. Main successful employees are women. We offer cultural, adventure, gastronomic, pilgrimage and different other tours. Also we can offer visa support, invitation letter, transport service, booking services by the request. Thanks to our staff we are able to offer you tours in Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan and Tajikistan.
